After careful research on the internet, leetcode is not just about solving the problems. It is about how effectively 
we can derive optimal solutions to the given problem.

When practicing leetcode, first timers make mistake of directly starting to solve the leetcode problems.

That is very wrong because if we do not understand the patterns, it becomes more like memorizing the problem than solving it correctly and knowledgably.

After analayzing the multiple leetcode questions, follwoing are the patterns that are observed inorder to solve the 
leetcode type of problems more efficiently and optimally.

The patterns are:
1. Sliding window
2. Two pointers
3. Dynamic programming
4. Breadth-First Search (BFS)
5. Depth-First Search (DFS)
6. Binary Search
7. Backtracking
8. Greedy algorithms
9. Divide and conquer
10. Topological sort
11. Union-find
12. Trie
13. Graph algorithms
14. Matrix manipulation
15. Bit manipulation

Note: The important thing is to understand the concepts and carefully observe the space and time complexity for the given problem, to derive the best optimal solution.
